About Us?

We are the Strathmore University IT Association, an umbrella body that covers all the IT groups in Strathmore University. We aim to provide members with the ability to acquire in demand skills and also provide career opportunities.

Meet the teams

Teams

Cybersecurity

The rate of cybercrime has grown exponentially. Come learn the skills to combat cybercrime with a number of courses and projects taken by the SUITSA cybersecurity team

Machine Learning and AI

Data is the new Gold! Come join the machine learning and AI team and learn how to create models that learn from data to do incredible things. Several courses will help you become a pro

IoT

The Internet of Things makes it easier to connect several devices together. This enables people to create interesting applications. Join the team and learn how to create your interesting projects
